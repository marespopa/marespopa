Who am I?

My name is Mareș Popa. I'm a software engineer and a marketing wizard, specializing in creating and planning engaging content on social media platforms. I live in a small town in northern Romania, Negrești Oaș.

I have over six years of experience as a programmer, being involved in the development of many products and projects throughout my career. I would describe myself as a programmer who is passionate about what he does, and curious to always be up to date with the news Web technologies❤️.

I started my web development journey in my first highschool year, when I created, alongside one of my classmates, an online football manager game, in which you can choose your own team, sell players, train them. That was my first contact with web applications and since then I have been hooked. ☀️
